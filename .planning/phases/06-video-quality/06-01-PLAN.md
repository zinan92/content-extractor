<objective>
Add volume normalization and VAD speech-ratio detection to the video extraction pipeline.

Purpose: Quiet recordings and non-speech audio (music, silence) degrade Whisper transcription quality. Normalizing volume before transcription and computing speech ratio from VAD metadata allows skipping non-speech content and improving transcript accuracy on real Douyin/XHS content.

Output: Updated ffmpeg.py with normalize_audio(), updated transcribe.py returning speech_ratio, updated VideoExtractor pipeline with normalization step and speech-ratio gating.
</objective>
